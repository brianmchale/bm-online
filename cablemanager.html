Cable Manager

A common inconvenience many people encounter in todayâ€™s tech-driven world is having a multitude of unorganized cables on their desk that create clutter and often slide off the desk when disconnected or not in use. The purpose of this project was to create a small, inexpensive, user-friendly product that would easily attach to any desk to allow for the organization of these cables, separating them and keeping them secured in place. This was successfully accomplished through our system of four spring-loaded cylindrical pins with openings to fit nearly any commonly-used charging cables. The springs allow for the pins to slide smoothly within their respective slots to expose the openings for the cables, and holes drilled into the base keep the springs in place and provide maximum compression.
